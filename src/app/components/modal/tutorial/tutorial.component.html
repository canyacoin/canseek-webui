Tutorial

An employer can create a job post and set up a bounty as reward to those refer good talents. Everyone can refer talents they know or even themselves to the post by paying a samll fee. All fees, together with the bounty will be added to a honeypot. As more talents get referred, the job post will reward more. And whichever talent gets selected finally, his/her recommender will win full amount of the honeypot.

How to add a job post?
  • 1. Own / Buy CAN
  • 2. Click "+ Job Post" button
  • 3. Fill in position details, set the bounty & application cost
  • 4. Submit the form and submit the transaction in the popped up MetaMask window.
  • 5. Wait and check MetaMask transaction status, and update the job post status or re-add the post.
How to refer a talent ?
  • 1. Own / Buy CAN
  • 2. Click "REFER" button
  • 3. Fill in talent details
  • 4. Submit the form and submit the transaction in the popped up MetaMask window.
  • 5. Wait and check MetaMask transaction status, and update the candidate status or re-refer the candidate.
How to close a job post?
  • 1. Select a candidate from the candidate list
  • 2. Pay transaction fee and platform fee (1%)
  • 3. Close the post and the recommender of the selected candidate gets the reward
How to cancel a job post?
  • 1. On the post you created, click the transh can icon
  • 2. Pay the transaction fee and cancellation fee (1%)
What if a job post gets cancelled?

You can claim the application cost by going to the cancelled tab and locate your post and candidate.
